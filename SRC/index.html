Problem:

The old company logo is visually and functionally outdated. Its elements did not correspond to modern design trends, and the fonts looked outdated.

Solution:

I redesigned the logo, and now it reflects the company's modern values and style. Updated fonts and a minimalistic sign make it current and easily recognizable. The new design is universal: the logo looks great digitally, on packaging, and as a stand-alone sticker.

Problem:

It is necessary to show on the main page of the site all the variety of Georgian wines.

Solution:

I created an illustration depicting the composition of glasses with specific brands of Georgian wine, which reflects the whole variety of shades — from white to red, with an emphasis on the characteristic amber tones of orange qvevri wines.

Problem:

It is necessary to create a bright visual navigation through the wine regions.

Solution:

I decided to present navigation through Georgia's wine regions as an interactive map, from the country's scale to the terroir. The map is drawn and programmed as SVG slides and linked to information from the site's database.
